# Video Tutorial Script: Teacher Content Management

**Duration**: 10-12 minutes  
**Target Audience**: Teachers  
**Difficulty**: Beginner to Intermediate

---

2. Understanding Your Permissions (0:45 - 2:00)

**[HIGHLIGHT: Content badge]**

### Tips and Best Practices (10:00 - 11:00)

**[SCREEN: Tips slide]**

**Narrator:**
"Here are some best practices for managing your courses effectively:"

**[TEXT OVERLAY: Content Management Tips]**
- Organize lessons in logical sequence
- Include diverse materials (videos, readings, activities)
- Test all links and materials before publishing
- Save drafts frequently
- Use clear, descriptive titles

**[TEXT OVERLAY: Grading Tips]**
- Grade submissions promptly (within 48 hours)
- Provide specific, constructive feedback
- Be consistent with grading criteria
- Use rubrics for objectivity
- Keep records of all grades

**[TEXT OVERLAY: Communication Tips]**
- Respond to messages within 24 hours
- Use professional language
- Be clear and concise
- Keep parents informed of important updates
- Use announcements for time-sensitive information

**[SCREEN: Coordination tip]**

**Narrator:**
"If you're working with other teachers on the same course, coordinate regularly. Communicate about content updates, grading responsibilities, and student concerns."

## Production Notes

### Equipment
- Screen recording software (Camtasia, ScreenFlow, or OBS)
- High-quality microphone
- Video editing software
- Sample course with test data

### Recording Tips
- Use a realistic test course
- Prepare sample content beforehand
- Show both successful and common scenarios
- Use smooth, deliberate mouse movements
- Pause between major sections

### Post-Production
- Add intro/outro animations
- Include subtle background music
- Add text overlays for key points
- Include captions/subtitles
- Add chapter markers for easy navigation
- Export in multiple formats

### Accessibility
- Include closed captions
- Provide full transcript
- Use high contrast for text
- Ensure clear audio
- Add audio descriptions
